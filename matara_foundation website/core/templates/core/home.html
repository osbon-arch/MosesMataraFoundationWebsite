Give Hope Through Education

Millions of children in Kenya still miss out on the basic right to education. About 1.8 million children between the ages of 6 and 18 have either dropped out of school or have never attended at all. Many come from poor households where the cost of uniforms, supplies, or transport is a heavy burden. Without urgent support, these children risk being left behind— not just in school, but in opportunities for health, livelihood, and full participation in society.

What We Do

Moses Matara Foundation is a non-profit organization committed to transforming lives through education in Kenya. Guided by values of compassion, integrity, and community, our mission is to provide vulnerable children with access to quality learning opportunities, resources, and mentorship. We believe that every child deserves the chance to learn, grow, and achieve their dreams. By empowering education today, we are building a brighter, more equitable tomorrow for generations to come.

Our Mission

“To empower vulnerable children in Kenya by providing access to quality education, mentorship, and resources that nurture their potential and create opportunities for a brighter future.”

At Matara Moses Foundation, our mission is to break the cycle of poverty by ensuring that every child, regardless of their background, has the chance to learn and thrive. We believe education is the foundation of empowerment and lasting change. Through school sponsorships, provision of learning materials, and mentorship programs, we strive to give children the tools they need to dream big, achieve their goals, and contribute positively to their communities. By investing in education today, we are shaping tomorrow’s leaders and building a brighter future for generations to come.
